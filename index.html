About CESA

Who We Are

CESA is the official student body of the Computer Engineering Department at ISBM College of Engineering. We strive to bridge the gap between academia and industry by organizing various technical and non-technical events.

Our Mission

To foster technical excellence, innovation, and leadership among computer engineering students through hands-on experience, industry exposure, and peer learning.
