Official Constitution and Guidelines of the Computer Engineering Students Association

CESA Constitution

Article I: Name and Purpose

The organization shall be known as the Computer Engineering Students Association (CESA) of ISBM College of Engineering.

The purpose of CESA is to:

  • Foster technical excellence among computer engineering students
  • Promote professional development and leadership skills
  • Create networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Organize technical and non-technical events

Article II: Membership

Membership is open to all computer engineering students of ISBM College of Engineering who:

  • Are currently enrolled in the computer engineering program
  • Maintain good academic standing
  • Pay the prescribed membership fees
  • Adhere to the CESA code of conduct
